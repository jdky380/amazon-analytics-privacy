Privacy Policy for Brand & Opportunity Analytics System

Application ID: AMZ-ANALYTICS-AUTO

Effective Date:

1. Overview

This privacy policy describes how our Amazon analytics application collects, uses, and protects data obtained through Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API).

2. Data Collection Scope

The application collects the following data types from Amazon Seller Central:

  • Brand Analytics: Search term reports, click share, conversion share metrics
  • Opportunity Explorer: Market insights, product opportunities, search term opportunities
  • Business Metrics: Performance data for internal analysis and reporting

3. Data Usage Purpose

All collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Internal business intelligence and performance analysis
  • Automated reporting and operational efficiency improvements
  • Market research and opportunity identification
  • Data-driven decision making for Amazon store management

4. Data Protection Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption (HTTPS/TLS 1.2+) for all data transmissions
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks
